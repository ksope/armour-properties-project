About Us

At NUKP, we believe in creating a brighter, better, healthier future for all citizens of the United Kingdom. We are a new political party founded on the principles of national progress and respect and justice for all citizens, through building a stronger, fairer, more just and prosperous nation, where everyone can feel the benefits and improvement to their own quality of life.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of integrity, absolute transparency, and accountability in all our endeavours. We are dedicated to serving the interests of the people first, before the pantomime, pouffe and individual financial profitability that plague the honour of current day politicians.

I know it is hard, almost impossible, to believe, but we are an honest party. Yes you scoff, but have you given us a chance?

All our representatives for parliamentary level are chosen on very few basic and interrelated qualities: their honesty, their human qualities and the inherent belief that all humans have a right to a dignified life within a prosperous nation such as ours. Unlike leading politicians of any present recognisable party, their souls are NOT for sale.

Our representatives are proud to be a part of this nation. We have come to the table not necessarily because we want to. But because we have to. I have had to trong arm many of the representatives towards politics. They have asked why? Simply because they have a character that the public domain need.

The absolute negative reputation of ALL politicians today has been the main reason many of our representatives have wanted to stay away from the word 'politicians.' I have simply told them, if you stay away, how do you expect the things you want changing to change?

We all have a duty to stand up in our nation to take on the awful people who choose themselves to represent us. This party was primarily born as not a single one of us feel there is anyone in today's politics that represents Britain today. We choose to change that. And stand up for the core goodness that we believe still exists in the grand nation of ours.

Meetings

We will host meetings on Discord. Anyone, and everyone is welcome to the meetings. Ane we mean everyone. People who are donors, non donors, members, non members, friends of the party, enemies of the party; we want everyone to attend. The more voices the more representative we are of the people. And we want that.

There is a caveat...yes I know you're thinking 'here we go...' But wait, and see if you agree! The caveat is we do demand courtesy from all attendees of any meetings. Anyone who thinks ill mannered behaviour is acceptable, will be removed from the meeting. We do not hope to remove ill mannered people forever; we know emotions can reach a boiling point when talking about highly emotive issues, but we also know that maybe a chat and some understanding with removed people will put us back on the same page where we can discuss issues politely no matter which angle we come at them from. We hope to engage positively with everyone.
